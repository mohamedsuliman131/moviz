## 📖 About

**Moviz** is a modern movie discovery app built with React. Browse popular movies & TV shows, search any title, watch trailers, and save your favorites to a personal Watchlist — all powered by TMDB API. Includes an AI chatbot that recommends movies based on your mood.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🏠 Hero section with popular movies & genre slider
- 🎥 Browse Movies, TV Shows & Trending content
- 🔍 Real-time search
- 🎬 Movie details with trailer, cast & ratings
- 📋 Personal Watchlist (requires login)
- 🔐 Register & Login with form validation
- 🤖 AI Chatbot (Arabic & English + Voice input)
